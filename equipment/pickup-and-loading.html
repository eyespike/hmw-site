Equipment Pickup & Loading

Local pickup only — we'll help you load it.

We have a tractor with forks on-site and are happy to assist with loading your equipment onto your trailer, truck, or other conveyance at your direction.

Before You Arrange Pickup
  • You direct the load We'll operate the tractor and forks as you instruct, but all rigging, positioning, and load decisions are yours.
  • We do not secure loads Strapping, chaining, blocking, and tie-down is your responsibility.
  • We do not hand-carry or manually lift heavy items The tractor and forks are what we have — plan accordingly.
  • Come prepared Bring appropriate strapping, load bars, blocking, and any special rigging your equipment requires.
  • Trailer capacity is your responsibility Know your trailer's weight rating before you arrive.

We want pickup to go smoothly for everyone. If you have questions about a specific piece of equipment's size or weight before scheduling, just ask.

Loading assistance is provided as a courtesy. Buyer assumes all responsibility for safe transport once equipment leaves the property.
